Hello World

2015-04-14 12:00 am

Hello, and welcome to my blog! My name is Erik Bergenholtz, and I am a security enthusiast and hacker living in Sweden. I hope to expand my website in the future, but for now this blog will have to do.

As I mentioned, I am a security enthusiast and hacker. I mostly enjoy computer security, but physical security such as burglary alarms and lock smithing is also quite amusing. This is what my blog will be about; security, hacking, projects and perhaps the odd contemplation.

I am not sure what else to write in this first post, so I’ll just leave it at that. I’m looking forward to sharing some of my projects, and I hope you’ll enjoy reading about them.

Until then, happy hacking!
