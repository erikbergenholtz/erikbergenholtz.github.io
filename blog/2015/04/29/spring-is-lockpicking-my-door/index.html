Spring Is Lockpicking My Door

2015-04-29 12:00 am

Spring is here, and for a beginner lockpicker this is great news! Why is it so? Because the streets are being cleaned. That means that machines with large with large steel (spring steel even, I believe) brushes are roaming the streets. This is good because all of a sudden the streets aren’t littered with just grovel, twigs and dust, they are also littered with (spring) steel brush “hairs” which happen to be the perfect size to make your own lockpicking tools. Unfortunately, I’ve only found three, but I will keep my eyes open.

The plan now is to get myself some metal files. I’ve found really cheap ones at eBay, the Lord and Saviour, and I plan to buy these. As they’re shipped from China it might take a while for them to come to Sweden, but I guess I’ll have to be patient. I plan to clean up the “hairs” while I wait, and also try making a tension tool, which really just requires a vice (which I already own).

So that’s pretty much it for now. I’ll write another post when I have made the tension tool and tried that out. Until then!

Happy Hacking!
