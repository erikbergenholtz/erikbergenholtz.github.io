Pump Up the SWAG

2015-05-31 12:00 am

They are here. The long wait has been ended. After a couple of months, I now sit here as a proud owner of Macetech’s brilliant invention the RGB-shades. The shades is a piece of wearable electronics which consists of a controller and an LED-matrix in the shape of a pair of shutter shades. They use the same microcontroller as an Arduino Pro, and can as such be programmed like one.

I have not spent too much time programming them so far, but I can tell you that they are awesome. I do not regret buying this one second, and I will enjoy hacking them. I plan on building a small circuit to make them follow the beat of the music at least, and hopefully I can come up with some other cool extension to them as well.

Meanwhile, enjoy my small “demo” of the patterns I’ve programmed so far.

Happy Hacking!
